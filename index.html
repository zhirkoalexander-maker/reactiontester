just me learning to code
started learning JavaScript and figured why not build something. this site is a reaction tester game thing.

use your keyboard or mouse to play. your scores get saved (yeah, localStorage is cool). each test lets you try 3 times.
What you can do here
Classic - fast clicks
wait for the green thing, click it. that's it
easy: 1-2 sec wait | medium: 1.5-2.5 sec | hard: 2-3.5 sec
Visual - spot the difference
theres circles and one blue dot. find it quick
easy: 6 circles total, 15 sec | medium: 9 circles, 10 sec | hard: 12 circles, 6 sec
Mental - math stuff
some math problems pop up. pick the right answer from 4 options
easy: basic math, you got 20 sec | medium: multiply & squares, 10 sec | hard: uh more complicated, 5 sec
Memory - the tricky one
i show you a number sequence, you gotta remember it and type it back. press 1-9 to answer
easy: 3 numbers, 3 sec to see them | medium: 4 numbers, 2 sec | hard: 5 numbers, 1 sec (pretty hard lol)
Chat - respond fast
i send you a message, type back quick. press Enter when done
easy: 20 sec | medium: 10 sec | hard: 5 sec. you gotta actually type something reasonable
Tracking - pure cursor control
no shooting here. circle shows up instantly, then you get a 3 second prep before it starts moving. keep your cursor on it and build track time
easy: a bit faster now | medium: faster again | hard: small + fast. best result = longest track time, not reaction
Target Burst - hits over speed
3 second prep, then timed burst. this one counts hits, not reaction. green target = point. miss = false start. on hard there is also a red target you must avoid
easy: 15 sec | medium: 12 sec | hard: 10 sec. your score is total hits in the time limit
Micro Adjust - tiny corrections
small targets spawn near the center so you train little aim corrections instead of big flicks
easy: larger dots and wider time | medium: smaller dots | hard: tiny dots and shorter windows. score = total hits
Target Switch - snap between targets
two targets appear at once. clear both fast to score one pair
easy: bigger pair with more time | medium: tighter window | hard: smaller targets and less time. score = completed pairs
controls:
• Space = click in Classic
• 1-9 = answer in Memory
• 1-4 = pick answer in Mental
• Enter = send in Chat
• ESC = exit test

your scores stick around (localStorage ftw), just pick a difficulty before you start
